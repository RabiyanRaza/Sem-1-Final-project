Car wash and deatiling service

We operate three car washes throught Portland area. Our goal is to provide our customers with the friendliest, most convenient hand car wash experience possible. We use the most modern and up-to-date water reclamation modules as a part of our car wash systems. Our products are eco-friendly.

  • We use professional equipment and have a fully trained staff
  • We make sure our customers are completely satisfied with their service
  • Professional detailing will increase the resale value of your car
  • We are a licensed and fully insured company
  • Book and pay for your wash electronically and securely
